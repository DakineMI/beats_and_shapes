# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT - BEATS & SHAPES

## Functional Requirements (Iteration 1: Core Experience)

### FR-01: Bullet-Hell Movement
- **Movement:** 1:1 touch-to-shape tracking.
- **Dashing:** Swipe gesture for invulnerable movement through obstacles.
- **Invulnerability:** Brief "mercy window" after taking damage.

### FR-02: 5 EDM Beat Levels
- **Levels:** 5 distinct EDM tracks with unique, rhythm-synced obstacle patterns.
- **Beat-Sync:** Every obstacle animation (pulsing, spawning, moving) must be locked to the track BPM.
- **The "Braxton" Metric:** 6-year-old can play and enjoy without frustration.

### FR-03: Visual Identity (The Clone Aesthetic)
- **Colors:** Deep black background, neon player shapes, bright pink obstacles.
- **Feedback:** Screen shake, color pulses, and shape "shattering" on hits.

### FR-04: Platform Basics
- **iPad Air 11" M2:** Optimized for 120Hz ProMotion displays.
- **Audio:** AVAudioEngine for zero-latency audio-visual sync.

## Iteration 2: Content & Progression
- **Levels:** Expansion to 20+ beats.
- **Bosses:** Large-scale rhythmic encounters synchronized with song drops.
- **Beat Editor:** Simple AI-assisted tools to create new levels.

## Iteration 3: Ecosystem
- **Music:** Full Apple Music catalog playback (hooks for DRM handling).
- **Sharing:** YouTube upload integration for gameplay clips.

## Performance Requirements
- **Latency:** <16ms input-to-screen.
- **Sync:** <2ms audio-to-visual drift.
- **Frame Rate:** Consistent 60/120fps.
